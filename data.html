Data Gathered :

According to the data gathered by the iNaturalist organization, Chalybion californicum commonly know as Nearctic Blue Mud-dauber Wasp were highly observed between the time of April to June in the United States of America. That makes the summertime in this location. With the help of data, it can be conveyed that summer is the most habitable time of the year for Chalybion californicum.



Chalybion californicum tends to have an average lifespan of 4 to 4 and a half months. These wasps are likely to be observed across the United States of America as most of the discovered Chalybion californicum were from the United states of America.
